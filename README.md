# Credit Risk Analysis for Small Business Loans

This project investigates which company and loan characteristics are most associated with default in a small business loan dataset.

## Objective

The goal of this project is to identify which business and loan characteristics are most associated with default and transform these findings into interpretable business insights.

## Key Questions

- What is the overall default rate in the dataset?
- Do smaller businesses present higher default risk?
- Are lower loan amounts associated with higher default?
- Does loan term influence risk?
- Is recession associated with higher default?
- Is it possible to build a simple risk score to classify contracts into low, medium, and high risk?
